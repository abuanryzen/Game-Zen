Background of the Study

  1. Website and Technology
    • WEBSITE
      • A website is a collection of interconnected web pages that are accessible through the internet using a web browser. Websites are used to share information, provide services, sell products, communicate with users, and much more. Every website has a unique address called a URL (Uniform Resource Locator), such as www.example.com. Websites can contain text, images, videos, forms, and interactive features. Industrial Arts(IA)
    • TECHNOLOGY
      • Technology refers to the application of scientific knowledge, tools, techniques, and systems to solve problems and make human life easier. It includes everything from simple tools and machines to advanced computers, smartphones, and artificial intelligence. Technology plays an important role in communication, education, healthcare, transportation, and business. It helps people work more efficiently, access information quickly, and connect with others around the world. In simple terms, technology is the use of knowledge and innovation to improve the way we live and work. Information Communication Technology(ICT)
  2. Importance of Websites:
    • 24/7 Accessibility: Information is available anytime and anywhere.
    • Global Reach: Businesses can connect with customers around the world.
    • Credibility and Professionalism: A website helps build trust and a professional image.
    • Marketing and Promotion: It is an effective platform for advertising products and services.
    • Customer Communication: Users can easily contact and interact with the organization.
